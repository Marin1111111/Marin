Prayer

A girl from an ancient tribe who has the mysterious ability to communicate with plants and seeds. She has an ageless face and an infinite life. She is revered by other people as a goddess who prays for the tribe and protects it from natural disasters. The giant installation behind the girl is a giant seed, made by the team at ST WORKSHOP 10.
